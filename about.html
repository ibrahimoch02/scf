Our Purpose

The Somali Cancer Foundation is established as a dedicated civic forum to organize efforts against cancer nationwide. The foundation actively supports cancer awareness campaigns and works tirelessly to connect patients, survivors, and concerned families with helpful clinical guidance, emotional support, and coordinates with available resources in local hospitals and healthcare centers. We believe that building early detection awareness saves lives in any community.

Supporting Somali Communities Collectively

By focusing on high-impact public events and printing educational guides on breast cancer, cervical cancer, and childhood oncology, we can alter path narratives. We coordinate with public volunteers and donors to scale up regional activities and enable more families in Somalia to get access to early screening guidelines.
