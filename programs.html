Cancer Awareness & Education

Educating communities about cancer signs, prevention awareness and the importance of seeking medical care early. We publish brochures and host public seminars.

Patient & Family Support

Offering guidance, emotional care, and compassionate support to patients affected by cancer. We facilitate counseling rooms to help families understand cancer.

Community Outreach

Organizing field visits, neighborhood assemblies, and cancer walks to raise critical funds and distribute awareness flyers across Mogadishu and surrounding towns.

Volunteer Activities

Engaging passionate community members, nursing students, and youth volunteers to support logistics at education workshops and deliver supportive messages.

Fundraising Support

Aggregating contributions locally and globally in order to subsidize oncological awareness guides and set up information tents in refugee camps and community hubs.

Partnerships

Liaising with Mogadishu healthcare clinics, national doctors, and international cancer support coalitions to promote cancer diagnosis protocols.

Empowering Communities Through Real Engagement

Our workshop seminars and awareness courses are carefully planned to reach urban neighborhoods and rural camps. By delivering information in local settings, we destigmatize the disease and build confidence in early modern clinical consultation over delay.
